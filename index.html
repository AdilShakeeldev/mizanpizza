Our Story

At Mizan, we believe in the power of authentic flavors and quality ingredients. Since 2018, we've been serving the Ballwin Manchester community with 100% halal pizzas that combine traditional recipes with modern creativity.

Every pizza is crafted with care using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and prepared according to halal standards. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a trusted name in halal dining.
